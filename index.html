about Bioshine
倍恩喜

Bioshine倍恩喜致力于提供安全高品质的新西兰羊奶产品，已经成为了乳制品和营养产品的领先品牌。

基于自然和科学，Bioshine倍恩喜使用创新的技术和原料，提高消费者的健康和福祉。

目前，倍恩喜已经有一系列产品出口往中国，越南，马来西亚，菲律宾，中东地区，中国香港和澳大利亚。

Bioshine is committed to providing safe and high-quality New Zealand sheep's milk products and has become a leading brand of dairy and nutritional products. Based on nature and science , Bioshine USES innovative technologies and ingredients to improve the health and well-being of consumers. Currently, bce exports a range of products to China, Vietnam, Malaysia , the Philippines , the Middle East , Hong Kong and Australia.
